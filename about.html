About Bright Reads

Bright Reads began as a family project to help parents and children discover books they could enjoy together. We believe great stories spark imagination, teach valuable lessons, and bring families closer through reading.

Our Mission

To provide honest, family-friendly book reviews that celebrate creativity while guiding parents toward age-appropriate, high-quality reads for kids ages 10–12.

Meet the Reviewers

  • Dad: Enjoys sci-fi and thought-provoking adventures.
  • Ella (age 12): A fantasy fan who loves dragons and heroes.
  • Ben (age 10): Prefers action and humor in every story.
