Redwing Enterprises Site Plan

Author: Andrew Lawrence

1. Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as the official home for Redwing Enterprises, showcasing the Redwing YouTube channel, its merchandise, and brand updates. It provides fans and visitors with a central hub to explore content, learn about the brand, and purchase merchandise.

2. Audience

The target audience includes fans and viewers of the Redwing YouTube channel, potential collaborators, and new visitors discovering the brand. The site will be designed to appeal to young adults and content creators interested in entertainment and branded merchandise.

3. Dynamic Elements (JavaScript)

This website will use JavaScript to add multiple dynamic elements. Examples include:

  • A dynamically loaded merch gallery generated from an array of item objects.
  • A theme switcher (light/dark mode) using DOM manipulation and event listeners.
  • A featured-video module that updates the embedded video based on user interaction.
  • Conditional logic to hide or reveal additional content sections.

JavaScript will use ES modules, objects, arrays, and array methods such as map, filter, and forEach.

7. Content

Home Page Content

Welcome to Redwing Enterprises, the official hub for the Redwing YouTube channel! Explore videos, updates, and exclusive merchandise designed for fans of the Redwing community. Stay connected and dive deeper into the content you love.

Child Page (Merch Page) Content

Browse our official Redwing merchandise collection. From apparel to accessories, each item reflects the Redwing brand and community spirit. All merch images, descriptions, and prices will load dynamically using JavaScript.
